Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximatelty 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper. to taste
  • 1 tablespoon if butter ar oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetabkesm cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: in a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed, You can add a tablespooon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan:Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette:Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Aff fillings (optional):When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the muddle. sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half os the omelette.
  5. Fild and serve:As the omelette continues to cook, carefilly lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy:Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
